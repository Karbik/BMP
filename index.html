WHAT IS THIS?

BMP is a toy? experiment? visualizer? based on Craig Reynolds's boids. He envisioned boids as "creatures" modelled to approximate flocking behaviour by following three simple rules:

  • Separation - steer to avoid crowding local flockmates
  • Alignment - steer towards the average heading of local flockmates
  • Cohesion - steer to move toward the average position of local flockmates
